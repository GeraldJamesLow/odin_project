Strawberry Daiquiri

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces frozen strawberries
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup lemon juice
  • ⅛ cup lime juice
  • ¾ cup rum
  • ¼ cup lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage
  • 6 cups ice, or as needed

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place frozen strawberries in a blender; add sugar, lemon juice, and lime juice.
  3. Pour in rum and lemon-lime beverage, then add ice.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Pour strawberry daiquiri mixture into hurricane glasses. Enjoy!
